Data Analysis
for the Modern Policy [maker / influencer]



Non-profits, government agencies, think tanks, and other policy-related organizations are collecting and analyzing more data than before - matching an established pattern among businesses over the last decade. This presents an opportunity to bring great influence to your field through simple, clever data analysis. But it has also raised expectations among employers for the ability to manipulate and understand data. This project aims to help IPS students and recent graduates in honing their data skills.
